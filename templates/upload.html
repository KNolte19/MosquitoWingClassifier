LandRoval - Mosquito Classifier

This service allows you to upload images of mosquito wings for species identification. Please note that this version is intended for testing and validation purposes. Although the classification model is reliable, there may be occasional misclassifications. Each prediction includes a confidence level metric to gauge the reliability of the classification. Upon submission, you will receive an identifier for later reference.

Upload Files

Images should have a neutral background and should depict a single complete wing with as little background as possible to acheive the best results. Upload an Image in the formats JPEG, PNG, TIFF. We encourge you to read the Image Capture Guidelines on how to remove mosquito wings and follow the instructions to capture an adequate image.
