CENEDRIL - Mosquito Classifier

This service allows you to upload images of mosquito wings for species identification. Please note that this is a beta version and be aware that no classification system is perfect, and occasional misclassifications may occur. For critical decisions, consult experts and additional resources. You will get an indication of models confidence attached to your results. For each request you will be given an identifier. After prediction you can either download only the prediction or the processed images.
